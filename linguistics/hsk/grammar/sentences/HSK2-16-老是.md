# 老是 (lǎoshì) — 二16

- **Grammar**: [HSK2-16-老是](../HSK2-16-老是.md)
- **Characters**: [老是](../characters/老是.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 别**老是**抱怨，做点实事吧。
  - *Bié **lǎoshì** bàoyuàn, zuò diǎn shíshì ba.*
  - Don't **always** complain; do something practical.

- 他**老是**把钥匙忘在家里。
  - *Tā **lǎoshì** bǎ yàoshi wàng zài jiālǐ.*
  - He **is forever** leaving his keys at home.

- 你为什么**老是**针对我？
  - *Nǐ wèishéme **lǎoshì** zhēnduì wǒ?*
  - Why are you **always** targeting me?

- 最近周末**老是**下雨，没法出去玩。
  - *Zuìjìn zhōumò **lǎoshì** xiàyǔ, méifǎ chūqù wán.*
  - It **keeps** raining on weekends lately; we can't go out to have fun.

- 妈妈**老是**担心我吃不好。
  - *Māma **lǎoshì** dānxīn wǒ chī bù hǎo.*
  - Mom is **constantly** worrying that I'm not eating well.
