# 以…为… (yǐ...wéi...) — HSK7-48

- **Grammar**: [HSK7-48-以…为…](../HSK7-48-以…为….md)
- **Characters**: [以…为…](../characters/以…为….md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 任何判断都必须==以==法律==为==准绳。
  - *Rènhé pànduàn dōu bìxū ==yǐ== fǎlǜ ==wéi== zhǔnshéng.*
  - Any judgment must ==take== the law ==as== the criterion.

- 这次会议==以==经济合作==为==主题。
  - *Zhè cì huìyì ==yǐ== jīngjì hézuò ==wéi== zhǔtí.*
  - This conference ==takes== economic cooperation ==as== its theme.

- 我们的午餐通常==以==米饭==为==主。
  - *Wǒmen de wǔcān tōngcháng ==yǐ== mǐfàn ==wéi== zhǔ.*
  - Our lunch usually ==consists== mainly ==of== rice.

- 医生==以==救死扶伤==为==己任。
  - *Yīshēng ==yǐ== jiùsǐ-fúshāng ==wéi== jǐrèn.*
  - Doctors ==regard== healing the wounded and rescuing the dying ==as== their own responsibility.

- 父母都==以==自己的孩子==为==傲。
  - *Fùmǔ dōu ==yǐ== zìjǐ de háizi ==wéi== ào.*
  - Parents are all ==proud of== their own children.
