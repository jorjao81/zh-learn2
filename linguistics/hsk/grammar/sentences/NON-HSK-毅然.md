# 毅然 (yìrán) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-毅然](../NON-HSK-毅然.md)
- **Characters**: [毅然](../characters/毅然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 尽管家人反对，他还是==毅然==选择了去偏远山区支教。
  - *Jǐnguǎn jiārén fǎnduì, tā háishì ==yìrán== xuǎnzé le qù piānyuǎn shānqū zhījiào.*
  - Despite his family's objection, he ==resolutely== chose to go to a remote mountain area to teach.

- 在国家危难时刻，许多年轻人==毅然==参军保卫祖国。
  - *Zài guójiā wēinàn shíkè, xǔduō niánqīngrén ==yìrán== cānjūn bǎowèi zǔguó.*
  - In the moment of national crisis, many young people ==without hesitation== joined the army to defend their motherland.

- 她==毅然==剪掉了留了多年的长发，象征着新的开始。
  - *Tā ==yìrán== jiǎndiào le liú le duōnián de chángfà, xiàngzhēng zhe xīn de kāishǐ.*
  - She ==decisively== cut off the long hair she had kept for years, symbolizing a new beginning.

- 面对巨大的金钱诱惑，他==毅然==拒绝了受贿。
  - *Miànduì jùdà de jīnqián yòuhuò, tā ==yìrán== jùjué le shòuhuì.*
  - Facing enormous monetary temptation, he ==firmly== refused the bribe.

- 当发现公司在做违法的事情时，他==毅然==选择了报警。
  - *Dāng fāxiàn gōngsī zài zuò wéifǎ de shìqing shí, tā ==yìrán== xuǎnzé le bàojǐng.*
  - When he discovered the company was doing illegal things, he ==resolutely== chose to call the police.
