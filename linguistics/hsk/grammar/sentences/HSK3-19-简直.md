# 简直 (jiǎnzhí) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-简直](../HSK3-19-简直.md)
- **Characters**: [简直](../characters/简直.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 外面的雨下得==简直==像倒下来一样。
  - *Wàimiàn de yǔ xià de jiǎnzhí xiàng dào xiàlai yíyàng.*
  - The rain outside is ==simply== pouring down (like it's being poured from a bucket).

- 这种行为==简直==就是抢劫！
  - *Zhè zhǒng xíngwéi jiǎnzhí jiùshì qiǎngjié!*
  - This kind of behavior is ==practically== robbery!

- 这道题太难了，我==简直==不知道该怎么开始。
  - *Zhè dào tí tài nán le, wǒ jiǎnzhí bù zhīdào gāi zěnme kāishǐ.*
  - This problem is too hard; I ==simply== don't know how to start.

- 他的回答==简直==太完美了，大家都鼓掌了。
  - *Tā de huídá jiǎnzhí tài wánměi le, dàjiā dōu gǔzhǎng le.*
  - His answer was ==simply== perfect; everyone applauded.

- 这儿吵得我==简直==无法工作。
  - *Zhèr chǎo de wǒ jiǎnzhí wúfǎ gōngzuò.*
  - It's so noisy here that I ==simply== cannot work.

- 这个故事==简直==太感人了，我都看哭了。
  - *Zhège gùshi jiǎnzhí tài gǎnrén le, wǒ dōu kàn kū le.*
  - This story is ==simply== too touching; I even cried.

- 他跑得太快了，==简直==像飞一样。
  - *Tā pǎo de tài kuài le, jiǎnzhí xiàng fēi yíyàng.*
  - He runs so fast; it's ==practically== like flying.
