# 反正 (fǎnzhèng) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-反正](../HSK3-19-反正.md)
- **Characters**: [反正](../characters/反正.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 不管你去不去，我==反正==是要去的。
  - *Bùguǎn nǐ qùbùqù, wǒ fǎnzhèng shì yào qù de.*
  - Regardless of whether you go or not, I'm going ==anyway==.

- 别人怎么说我不管，==反正==我喜欢。
  - *Biérén zěnme shuō wǒ bùguǎn, fǎnzhèng wǒ xǐhuān.*
  - I don't care what others say; ==anyway== I like it.

- 别犹豫了，==反正==这东西不贵，买就买吧。
  - *Bié yóuyù le, fǎnzhèng zhè dōngxī bù guì, mǎi jiù mǎi ba.*
  - Stop hesitating; ==anyway== this thing isn't expensive, just buy it.

- ==反正==我们已经迟到了，不如慢慢走吧。
  - *Fǎnzhèng wǒmen yǐjīng chídào le, bùrú mànmàn zǒu ba.*
  - ==In any case== we are already late, so we might as well walk slowly.

- 便宜还是贵，==反正==我现在没钱买。
  - *Piányi háishì guì, fǎnzhèng wǒ xiànzài méiqiánmǎi.*
  - Cheap or expensive, ==anyway== I have no money to buy it now.
