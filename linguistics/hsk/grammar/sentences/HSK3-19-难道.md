# 难道 (nándào) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-难道](../HSK3-19-难道.md)
- **Characters**: [难道](../characters/难道.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 都这么晚了，==难道==你还要出去吗？
  - *Dōu zhèmewǎn le, nándào nǐ háiyào chūqù ma?*
  - It's so late; ==don't tell me== you are still going out?

- ==难道==这就是你要找的那本书？
  - *Nándào zhè jiùshì nǐ yào zhǎo de nàběnshū?*
  - ==Could it be that== this is the book you are looking for?

- 这么多菜，==难道==我们几个人吃得完吗？
  - *Zhème duōcài, nándào wǒmen jǐgè rén chīdéwán ma?*
  - ==Do you really think== we few people can finish so many dishes?

- 他没接电话，==难道==是生我的气了？
  - *Tā méi jiēdiànhuà, nándào shì shēng wǒ de qì le?*
  - He didn't answer the phone; ==could it be that== he is angry with me?

- 我都说了对不起，==难道==你还不原谅我吗？
  - *Wǒ dōu shuō le duìbùqǐ, nándào nǐ hái bù yuánliàng wǒ ma?*
  - I've already said sorry; ==don't tell me== you still won't forgive me?
