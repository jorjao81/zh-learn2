# 在于 (zàiyú) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-在于](../NON-HSK-在于.md)
- **Characters**: [在于](../characters/在于.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 失败的原因往往==在于==准备不足。
  - *Shībài de yuányīn wǎngwǎng ==zàiyú== zhǔnbèi bùzú.*
  - The reason for failure often ==lies in== insufficient preparation.

- 这本书的价值==在于==它真实地记录了历史。
  - *Zhè běn shū de jiàzhí ==zàiyú== tā zhēnshí de jìlù le lìshǐ.*
  - The value of this book ==lies in== its truthful recording of history.

- 这个工作的困难==在于==需要很多耐心。
  - *Zhège gōngzuò de kùnnán ==zàiyú== xūyào hěnduō nàixīn.*
  - The difficulty of this job ==lies in== requiring a lot of patience.

- 决定权==在于==你自己。
  - *Juédìngquán ==zàiyú== nǐ zìjǐ.*
  - The power to decide ==rests with== yourself.

- 安全的关键==在于==预防。
  - *Ānquán de guānjiàn ==zàiyú== yùfáng.*
  - The key to safety ==lies in== prevention.
