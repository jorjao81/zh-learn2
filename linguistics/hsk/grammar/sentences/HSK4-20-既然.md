# 既然 (jìrán) — 四20

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-20-既然](../HSK4-20-既然.md)
- **Characters**: [既然](../characters/既然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- ==既然==经理同意了，那我们就马上开始吧。
  - *Jìrán jīnglǐ tóngyì le, nà wǒmen jiù mǎshàng kāishǐ ba.*
  - ==Since== the manager agreed, let's start immediately.

- ==既然==你生病了，就在家好好休息吧。
  - *Jìrán nǐ shēngbìng le, jiù zài jiā hǎohǎo xiūxi ba.*
  - ==Since== you are sick, just rest well at home.

- 事情==既然==已经发生了，后悔也没用。
  - *Shìqing jìrán yǐjīng fāshēng le, hòuhuǐ yě méi yòng.*
  - ==Since== the thing has already happened, regretting it is useless.

- ==既然==票都卖完了，我们只好换个时间去。
  - *Jìrán piào dōu màiwán le, wǒmen zhǐhǎo huàn gè shíjiān qù.*
  - ==Since== the tickets are all sold out, we have to go at another time.

- ==既然==答应了他，你就不应该反悔。
  - *Jìrán dāying le tā, nǐ jiù bù yīnggāi fǎnhuǐ.*
  - ==Since== you promised him, you shouldn't go back on your word.
