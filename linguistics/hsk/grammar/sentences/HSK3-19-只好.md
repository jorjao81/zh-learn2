# 只好 (zhǐhǎo) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-只好](../HSK3-19-只好.md)
- **Characters**: [只好](../characters/只好.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 大家都不同意，我==只好==放弃这个想法。
  - *Dàjiā dōu bù tóngyì, wǒ zhǐhǎo fàngqì zhège xiǎngfǎ.*
  - Everyone disagreed, so I ==had no choice but to== give up this idea.

- 冰箱里没有吃的了，我们==只好==叫外卖。
  - *Bīngxiāng li méiyǒu chī de le, wǒmen zhǐhǎo jiào wàimài.*
  - There's no food in the fridge, so we ==have to== order takeout.

- 没有直达的飞机，我==只好==在上海转机。
  - *Méiyǒu zhídá de fēijī, wǒ zhǐhǎo zài Shànghǎi zhuǎnjī.*
  - There are no direct flights, so I ==am forced to== transfer in Shanghai.

- 因为忘了带钥匙，我==只好==在门外等家人回来。
  - *Yīnwèi wàngle dài yàoshi, wǒ zhǐhǎo zài ménwài děng jiārén huílái.*
  - Because I forgot to bring the key, I ==had to== wait outside for my family to return.

- 电脑突然坏了，我==只好==用手机写作业。
  - *Diànnǎo tūrán huài le, wǒ zhǐhǎo yòng shǒujī xiě zuòyè.*
  - The computer suddenly broke, so I ==had to== use my phone to do the homework.

- 没买到回家的火车票，我==只好==坐大巴。
  - *Méi mǎi dào huí jiā de huǒchē piào, wǒ zhǐhǎo zuò dàbā.*
  - I didn't manage to buy a train ticket home, so I ==had no choice but to== take the bus.

- 电脑还没修好，我==只好==先看书。
  - *Diànnǎo hái méi xiūhǎo, wǒ zhǐhǎo xiān kàn shū.*
  - The computer isn't fixed yet, so I ==have to== read a book first.
