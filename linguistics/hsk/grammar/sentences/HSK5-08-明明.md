# 明明 (míngmíng) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-明明](../HSK5-08-明明.md)
- **Characters**: [明明](../characters/明明.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- ==明明==是绿灯，那辆车为什么不走？
  - *==Míngmíng== shì lǜdēng, nà liàng chē wèishénme bù zǒu?*
  - It is ==obviously== a green light; why isn't that car moving?

- 钥匙==明明==就在桌子上，我怎么刚才没看见？
  - *Yàoshi ==míngmíng== jiù zài zhuōzi shang, wǒ zěnme gāngcái méi kànjiàn?*
  - The keys were ==plainly== right on the table; how come I didn't see them just now?

- 他==明明==答应了今天要来，怎么到现在还没影儿？
  - *Tā ==míngmíng== dāying le jīntiān yào lái, zěnme dào xiànzài hái méi yǐngr?*
  - He ==clearly== promised to come today; how come there is still no sign of him?

- 这道题==明明==老师讲过，你为什么还是做错了？
  - *Zhè dào tí ==míngmíng== lǎoshī jiǎng guò, nǐ wèishénme háishì zuò cuò le?*
  - The teacher ==obviously== explained this problem; why did you still get it wrong?

- 我==明明==把钱放在钱包里了，怎么不见了？
  - *Wǒ ==míngmíng== bǎ qián fàng zài qiánbāo li le, zěnme bújiàn le?*
  - I ==definitely== put the money in the wallet; how did it disappear?
