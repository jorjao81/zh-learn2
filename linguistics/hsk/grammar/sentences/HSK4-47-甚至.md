# 甚至 (shènzhì) — 四47

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-47-甚至](../HSK4-47-甚至.md)
- **Characters**: [甚至](../characters/甚至.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 这个问题太难了，==甚至==连老师都不会。
  - *Zhège wèntí tài nán le, shènzhì lián lǎoshī dōu bú huì.*
  - This problem is too hard; ==even== the teacher doesn't know it.

- 他去过很多国家，==甚至==去过南极。
  - *Tā qùguo hěnduō guójiā, shènzhì qùguo Nánjí.*
  - He has been to many countries, ==even== to Antarctica.

- 她很生气，==甚至==不想跟我说话。
  - *Tā hěn shēngqì, shènzhì bù xiǎng gēn wǒ shuōhuà.*
  - She is very angry; she ==even== doesn't want to talk to me.

- 我不认识他，==甚至==不知道他的名字。
  - *Wǒ bú rènshi tā, shènzhì bù zhīdào tā de míngzi.*
  - I don't know him; I ==even== don't know his name.

- 这台电脑很旧，==甚至==打不开网页。
  - *Zhè tái diànnǎo hěn jiù, shènzhì dǎbùkāi wǎngyè.*
  - This computer is very old; it ==even== cannot open web pages.
