# 当然 (dāngrán) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-当然](../HSK3-19-当然.md)
- **Characters**: [当然](../characters/当然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我==当然==知道他不来了，他早就告诉我了。
  - *Wǒ dāngrán zhīdào tā bù lái le, tā zǎojiù gàosu wǒ le.*
  - I ==of course== know he isn't coming; he told me long ago.

- 如果他努力工作，老板给他加薪是==当然==的。
  - *Rúguǒ tā nǔlì gōngzuò, lǎobǎn gěi tā jiāxīn shì dāngrán de.*
  - If he works hard, it is ==only natural== that the boss gives him a raise.

- 这款手机==当然==好，但是价格太贵了。
  - *Zhè kuǎn shǒujī dāngrán hǎo, dànshì jiàgé tài guì le.*
  - This mobile phone is ==of course== good, but the price is too expensive.

- 父母关心孩子是理所==当然==的。
  - *Fùmǔ guānxīn háizi shì lǐsuǒdāngrán de.*
  - It is a matter of ==course== (only natural) for parents to care about their children.

- 你帮了我这么大的忙，我==当然==要请你吃饭。
  - *Nǐ bāng le wǒ zhème dà de máng, wǒ dāngrán yào qǐng nǐ chīfàn.*
  - You helped me so much; I ==certainly== should invite you to dinner.

- 既然你请客，我==当然==要去吃大餐。
  - *Jìrán nǐ qǐngkè, wǒ dāngrán yào qù chī dàcān.*
  - Since you're treating, I ==of course== want to eat a big meal.

- 这点小事，你==当然==办得到。
  - *Zhè diǎn xiǎoshì, nǐ dāngrán bàn dé dào.*
  - This small matter, you ==certainly== can handle it.
