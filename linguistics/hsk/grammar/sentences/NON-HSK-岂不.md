# 岂不 (qǐbù) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-岂不](../NON-HSK-岂不.md)
- **Characters**: [岂不](../characters/岂不.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 你若不解释清楚，他==岂不==会误会？
  - *Nǐ ruò bù jiěshì qīngchu, tā qǐbù huì wùhuì?*
  - If you don't explain clearly, ==wouldn't== he misunderstand?

- 住在这里离公司近，房租又便宜，==岂不==很方便？
  - *Zhù zài zhèlǐ lí gōngsī jìn, fángzū yòu piányi, qǐbù hěn fāngbiàn?*
  - Living here is close to the office and the rent is cheap; ==isn't that== very convenient?

- 我们帮他作弊，==岂不==是反而害了他？
  - *Wǒmen bāng tā zuòbì, qǐbù shì fǎn'ér hài le tā?*
  - If we help him cheat, ==aren't we== actually harming him?

- 既然大家都同意，我们马上行动==岂不==痛快？
  - *Jìrán dàjiā dōu tóngyì, wǒmen mǎshàng xíngdòng qǐbù tòngkuai?*
  - Since everyone agrees, ==wouldn't it be== satisfying to act immediately?

- 他乡遇故知，==岂不==是一件乐事？
  - *Tāxiāng yù gùzhī, qǐbù shì yī jiàn lèshì?*
  - Meeting an old friend in a foreign land, ==isn't that== a joyful thing?
