# 而 (ér) — 四20

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-20-而](../HSK4-20-而.md)
- **Characters**: [而](../characters/而.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 这种花在北方很常见，==而==在南方很少见。
  - *Zhè zhǒng huā zài běifāng hěn chángjiàn, ér zài nánfāng hěn shǎojiàn.*
  - This kind of flower is very common in the north, ==while== it is rare in the south.

- 我喜欢安静，==而==他喜欢热闹。
  - *Wǒ xǐhuan ānjìng, ér tā xǐhuan rènao.*
  - I like peace and quiet, ==whereas== he likes bustle.

- 很多人想成功，==而==不愿意付出努力。
  - *Hěnduō rén xiǎng chénggōng, ér bú yuànyì fùchū nǔlì.*
  - Many people want to succeed, ==yet== are unwilling to put in the effort.

- 那个故事美丽==而==感人。
  - *Nàge gùshi měilì ér gǎnrén.*
  - That story is beautiful ==and== moving.

- 他是一位严肃==而==认真的老师。
  - *Tā shì yí wèi yánsù ér rènzhēn de lǎoshī.*
  - He is a serious ==and== conscientious teacher.
