# 总之 (zǒngzhī) — 四20

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-20-总之](../HSK4-20-总之.md)
- **Characters**: [总之](../characters/总之.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 天气不好，我也没空，==总之==今天的活动取消了。
  - *Tiānqì bù hǎo, wǒ yě méi kòng, zǒngzhī jīntiān de huódòng qǔxiāo le.*
  - The weather is bad, and I don't have time; ==in short==, today's activity is canceled.

- 你可以坐公交车或者地铁，==总之==怎么方便怎么去。
  - *Nǐ kěyǐ zuò gōngjiāochē huòzhě dìtiě, zǒngzhī zěnme fāngbiàn zěnme qù.*
  - You can take the bus or the subway; ==anyhow==, go however is convenient.

- 虽然过程很辛苦，但结果很好，==总之==我们成功了。
  - *Suīrán guòchéng hěn xīnkǔ, dàn jiéguǒ hěn hǎo, zǒngzhī wǒmen chénggōng le.*
  - Although the process was hard, the result is good; ==in conclusion==, we succeeded.

- 我很忙，很累，也没钱，==总之==我不能跟你去旅游。
  - *Wǒ hěn máng, hěn lèi, yě méi qián, zǒngzhī wǒ bù néng gēn nǐ qù lǚyóu.*
  - I am busy, tired, and have no money; ==in short==, I can't go traveling with you.

- 这个计划有风险，成本也高，==总之==不太可行。
  - *Zhège jìhuà yǒu fēngxiǎn, chéngběn yě gāo, zǒngzhī bú tài kěxíng.*
  - This plan has risks and high costs; ==in short==, it's not very feasible.
