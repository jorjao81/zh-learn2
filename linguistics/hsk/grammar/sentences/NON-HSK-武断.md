# 武断 (wǔduàn) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-武断](../NON-HSK-武断.md)
- **Characters**: [武断](../characters/武断.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 没有调查就下结论，这样做太==武断==了。
  - *Méiyǒu diàochá jiù xià jiélùn, zhèyàng zuò tài ==wǔduàn== le.*
  - Making a conclusion without investigation is too ==arbitrary==.

- 凭外表判断一个人是很==武断==的。
  - *Píng wàibiǎo pànduàn yí ge rén shì hěn ==wǔduàn== de.*
  - Judging a person by their appearance is very ==subjective==.

- 你不能==武断==地认为所有人都会同意你的观点。
  - *Nǐ bùnéng ==wǔduàn== de rènwéi suǒyǒu rén dōu huì tóngyì nǐ de guāndiǎn.*
  - You cannot ==arbitrarily== assume that everyone will agree with your point of view.

- 他那个==武断==的决定给我们造成了很大的损失。
  - *Tā nà ge ==wǔduàn== de juédìng gěi wǒmen zàochéng le hěn dà de sǔnshī.*
  - That ==rash== decision of his caused us great losses.

- 这种说法未免太==武断==了，缺乏科学依据。
  - *Zhè zhǒng shuōfǎ wèimiǎn tài ==wǔduàn== le, quēfá kēxué yījù.*
  - This statement is rather ==dogmatic== and lacks scientific basis.
