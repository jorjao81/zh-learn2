## Sentence Exercises

- 这次活动虽然累，但大家==毕竟==都很开心。
  - *Zhè cì huódòng suīrán lèi, dàn dàjiā ==bìjìng== dōu hěn kāixīn.*
  - Although this activity was tiring, everyone was ==after all== very happy.

- 孩子==毕竟==年纪小，不懂这些道理是正常的。
  - *Háizi ==bìjìng== niánjì xiǎo, bù dǒng zhèxiē dàolǐ shì zhèngcháng de.*
  - The child is ==after all== young; it is normal that he doesn't understand these principles.

- 他==毕竟==是专业的，做起事来很有经验。
  - *Tā ==bìjìng== shì zhuānyè de, zuò qǐ shì lái hěn yǒu jīngyàn.*
  - He is ==after all== a professional, so he is very experienced when doing things.

- 便宜货==毕竟==是便宜货，质量不能跟名牌比。
  - *Piányi huò ==bìjìng== shì piányi huò, zhìliàng bù néng gēn míngpái bǐ.*
  - Cheap goods are ==after all== cheap goods; the quality cannot compare with name brands.

- 不管怎么说，家==毕竟==是最温暖的地方。
  - *Bùguǎn zěnme shuō, jiā ==bìjìng== shì zuì wēnnuǎn de dìfang.*
  - No matter what is said, home is ==after all== the warmest place.
