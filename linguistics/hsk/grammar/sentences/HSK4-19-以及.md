# 以及 (yǐjí) — 四19

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-19-以及](../HSK4-19-以及.md)
- **Characters**: [以及](../characters/以及.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我们需要邀请亲戚、朋友==以及==同事。
  - *Wǒmen xūyào yāoqǐng qīnqi, péngyou ==yǐjí== tóngshì.*
  - We need to invite relatives, friends, ==as well as== colleagues.

- 这本书记载了这个国家的历史、文化==以及==风俗习惯。
  - *Zhè běn shū jìzǎi le zhè gè guójiā de lìshǐ, wénhuà ==yǐjí== fēngsú xíguàn.*
  - This book records the history, culture, ==and== customs of this country.

- 报名时请带上身份证、护照==以及==两张照片。
  - *Bàomíng shí qǐng dài shàng shēnfènzhèng, hùzhào ==yǐjí== liǎng zhāng zhàopiàn.*
  - Please bring your ID card, passport, ==and== two photos when registering.

- 我们要分析问题产生的原因、经过==以及==结果。
  - *Wǒmen yào fēnxī wèntí chǎnshēng de yuányīn, jīngguò ==yǐjí== jiéguǒ.*
  - We need to analyze the cause, process, ==as well as== the result of the problem.

- 由于天气==以及==交通的原因，我也许会晚到。
  - *Yóuyú tiānqì ==yǐjí== jiāotōng de yuányīn, wǒ yěxǔ huì wǎn dào.*
  - Due to the weather ==as well as== traffic reasons, I might arrive late.
