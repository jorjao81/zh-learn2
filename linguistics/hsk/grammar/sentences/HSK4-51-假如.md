# 假如 (jiǎrú) — 四51

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-51-假如](../HSK4-51-假如.md)
- **Characters**: [假如](../characters/假如.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- ==假如==你遇到这种情况，你会怎么办？
  - *Jiǎrú nǐ yùdào zhè zhǒng qíngkuàng, nǐ huì zěnme bàn?*
  - ==If== you encountered this situation, what would you do?

- ==假如==有机会出国留学，你想去哪个国家？
  - *Jiǎrú yǒu jīhuì chūguó liúxué, nǐ xiǎng qù nǎge guójiā?*
  - ==Supposing== you had the opportunity to study abroad, which country would you want to go to?

- ==假如==我是超人，我要帮助所有的人。
  - *Jiǎrú wǒ shì Chāorén, wǒ yào bāngzhù suǒyǒu de rén.*
  - ==If== I were Superman, I would help everyone.

- ==假如==没有手机，生活会变成什么样？
  - *Jiǎrú méiyǒu shǒujī, shēnghuó huì biànchéng shénme yàng?*
  - ==Supposing== there were no mobile phones, what would life become like?

- 我们必须现在出发，==假如==晚了就赶不上火车了。
  - *Wǒmen bìxū xiànzài chūfā, jiǎrú wǎn le jiù gǎnbúshàng huǒchē le.*
  - We must leave now; ==if== we are late, we won't catch the train.
