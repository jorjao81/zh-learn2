# 对于 (duìyú) — 四14

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-14-对于](../HSK4-14-对于.md)
- **Characters**: [对于](../characters/对于.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- ==对于==这个问题，我有不同的看法。
  - *Duìyú zhège wèntí, wǒ yǒu bùtóng de kànfǎ.*
  - ==Regarding== this problem, I have a different view.

- 老师==对于==每个学生都很关心。
  - *Lǎoshī duìyú měi gè xuéshēng dōu hěn guānxīn.*
  - The teacher is very concerned ==about== every student.

- 这种药==对于==感冒很有用。
  - *Zhè zhǒng yào duìyú gǎnmào hěn yǒuyòng.*
  - This medicine is very useful ==for== colds.

- ==对于==工作，他一直非常认真。
  - *Duìyú gōngzuò, tā yīzhí fēicháng rènzhēn.*
  - ==When it comes to== work, he has always been very serious.

- 大家==对于==他的决定表示支持。
  - *Dàjiā duìyú tā de juédìng biǎoshì zhīchí.*
  - Everyone expressed support ==regarding== his decision.
