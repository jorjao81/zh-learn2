# 不免 (bùmiǎn) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-不免](../HSK5-08-不免.md)
- **Characters**: [不免](../characters/不免.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 第一次上台演讲，心里==不免==感到紧张。
  - *Dì-yī cì shàngtái yǎnjiǎng, xīnli ==bùmiǎn== gǎndào jǐnzhāng.*
  - Going on stage to give a speech for the first time, one ==inevitably== feels nervous.

- 看见别人都成功了，他心里==不免==有些着急。
  - *Kànjiàn biéren dōu chénggōng le, tā xīnli ==bùmiǎn== yǒuxiē zháojí.*
  - Seeing that others have succeeded, he ==inevitably== feels a bit anxious.

- 这么多工作一个人做，==不免==会觉得吃力。
  - *Zhème duō gōngzuò yí gè rén zuò, ==bùmiǎn== huì juéde chīlì.*
  - Doing so much work alone, one will ==unavoidably== feel it is strenuous.

- 那个孩子从小离开父母，性格==不免==有些孤僻。
  - *Nàge háizi cóngxiǎo líkāi fùmǔ, xìnggé ==bùmiǎn== yǒuxiē gūpì.*
  - That child left his parents at a young age, so his personality is ==inevitably== a bit withdrawn.

- 大家都喝了不少酒，说话==不免==大声了一些。
  - *Dàjiā dōu hē le bùshǎo jiǔ, shuōhuà ==bùmiǎn== dàshēng le yìxiē.*
  - Everyone drank quite a bit, so they ==unavoidably== spoke a bit louder.
