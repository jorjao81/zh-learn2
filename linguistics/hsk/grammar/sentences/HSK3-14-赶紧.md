# 赶紧 (gǎnjǐn) — 三14

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-14-赶紧](../HSK3-14-赶紧.md)
- **Characters**: [赶紧](../characters/赶紧.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 趁超市还没关门，你==赶紧==去买点牛奶吧。
  - *Chèn chāoshì hái méi guānmén, nǐ ==gǎnjǐn== qù mǎi diǎn niúnǎi ba.*
  - While the supermarket isn't closed yet, you'd better ==hurry== and go buy some milk.

- 这件事不能再拖了，我们要==赶紧==想个办法。
  - *Zhè jiàn shì bùnéng zài tuō le, wǒmen yào ==gǎnjǐn== xiǎng gè bànfǎ.*
  - We can't delay this matter any longer; we need to ==hurriedly== think of a solution.

- 看到有人摔倒了，大家==赶紧==过去把他扶起来。
  - *Kàndào yǒurén shuāidǎo le, dàjiā ==gǎnjǐn== guòqù bǎ tā fú qǐlai.*
  - Seeing someone fall, everyone ==immediately== went over to help him up.

- 既然错了，就==赶紧==改正，不要找借口。
  - *Jìrán cuò le, jiù ==gǎnjǐn== gǎizhèng, búyào zhǎo jièkǒu.*
  - Since you are wrong, ==quickly== correct it and don't make excuses.

- 只有最后几张票了，你==赶紧==上网订吧。
  - *Zhǐyǒu zuìhòu jǐ zhāng piào le, nǐ ==gǎnjǐn== shàngwǎng dìng ba.*
  - There are only a few tickets left; you should ==hurry up== and book them online.
