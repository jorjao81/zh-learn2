# 此外 (cǐwài) — 四20

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-20-此外](../HSK4-20-此外.md)
- **Characters**: [此外](../characters/此外.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 想要身体健康，你每天要坚持运动。==此外==，还要多吃新鲜蔬菜。
  - *Xiǎngyào shēntǐ jiànkāng, nǐ měitiān yào jiānchí yùndòng. Cǐwài, hái yào duō chī xīnxiān shūcài.*
  - If you want to be healthy, you need to exercise every day. ==In addition==, you should eat more fresh vegetables.

- 这里的风景非常漂亮。==此外==，当地人也很热情。
  - *Zhèlǐ de fēngjǐng fēicháng piàoliang. Cǐwài, dāngdìrén yě hěn rèqíng.*
  - The scenery here is very beautiful. ==Moreover==, the locals are also very enthusiastic.

- 这份工作要求你会说英语。==此外==，最好有两年的工作经验。
  - *Zhè fèn gōngzuò yāoqiú nǐ huì shuō Yīngyǔ. Cǐwài, zuìhǎo yǒu liǎng nián de gōngzuò jīngyàn.*
  - This job requires you to speak English. ==Besides==, it is best to have two years of work experience.

- 房租是每月三千元。==此外==，你需要自己付水电费。
  - *Fángzū shì měiyuè sānqiān yuán. Cǐwài, nǐ xūyào zìjǐ fù shuǐdiànfèi.*
  - The rent is 3,000 yuan per month. ==Apart from this==, you need to pay for water and electricity yourself.

- 复习课文很重要。==此外==，多跟中国朋友聊天也能提高水平。
  - *Fùxí kèwén hěn zhòngyào. Cǐwài, duō gēn Zhōngguó péngyǒu liáotiān yě néng tígāo shuǐpíng.*
  - Reviewing the text is very important. ==In addition==, chatting more with Chinese friends can also improve your level.
