# 其实 (qíshí) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-其实](../HSK3-19-其实.md)
- **Characters**: [其实](../characters/其实.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 他表面上很凶，==其实==心里很善良。
  - *Tā biǎomiàn shàng hěn xiōng, qíshí xīnlǐ hěn shànliáng.*
  - He appears fierce on the surface, but ==actually== he is kind-hearted inside.

- 我说我不饿，==其实==我已经饿坏了。
  - *Wǒ shuō wǒ bù è, qíshí wǒ yǐjīng èhuài le.*
  - I said I wasn't hungry, but ==actually== I was starving.

- 很多人觉得汉语难学，==其实==只要有耐心，并没有那么难。
  - *Hěnduō rén juéde hànyǔ nán xué, qíshí zhǐyào yǒu nàixīn, bìng méiyǒu nàme nán.*
  - Many people think Chinese is hard to learn, but ==in fact== if you have patience, it's not that hard.

- ==其实==我并不想去，但是不得不去。
  - *Qíshí wǒ bìng bùxiǎng qù, dànshì bùdébù qù.*
  - ==Truth be told==, I don't want to go, but I have to.

- 你不用担心，这个困难==其实==很容易解决。
  - *Nǐ bùyòng dānxīn, zhège kùnnán qíshí hěn róngyì jiějué.*
  - You don't need to worry; this difficulty is ==actually== very easy to resolve.
