# 尽管 (jǐnguǎn) — 五45

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-45-尽管](../HSK5-45-尽管.md)
- **Characters**: [尽管](../characters/尽管.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- ==尽管==外面下着大雨，他还是准时来了。
  - *==Jǐnguǎn== wàimiàn xiàzhe dàyǔ, tā háishì zhǔnshí lái le.*
  - ==Even though== it was raining heavily outside, he still came on time.

- ==尽管==这次考试很难，但是我通过了。
  - *==Jǐnguǎn== zhè cì kǎoshì hěn nán, dànshì wǒ tōngguò le.*
  - ==Although== this exam was very difficult, I passed.

- ==尽管==我们意见不同，但我们仍然是朋友。
  - *==Jǐnguǎn== wǒmen yìjiàn bùtóng, dàn wǒmen réngrán shì péngyou.*
  - ==Despite== our differences in opinion, we are still friends.

- 他==尽管==很累，却睡不着。
  - *Tā ==jǐnguǎn== hěn lèi, què shuìbuzháo.*
  - ==Even though== he is very tired, he can't fall asleep.

- ==尽管==大家都反对，他还是决定去试一试。
  - *==Jǐnguǎn== dàjiā dōu fǎnduì, tā háishì juédìng qù shìyíshì.*
  - ==Although== everyone opposed it, he still decided to give it a try.
