# 白 (bái) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-白](../HSK3-19-白.md)
- **Characters**: [白](../characters/白.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 如果你不去考试，你这学期的努力就==白==费了。
  - _Rúguǒ nǐ bú qù kǎoshì, nǐ zhè xuéqī de nǔlì jiù báifèi le._
  - If you don't take the exam, your efforts this semester will be ==in vain==.

- 他忙了一整天，结果什么都没做成，真是==白==忙了。
  - _Tā máng le yì zhěng tiān, jiéguǒ shénme dōu méi zuòchéng, zhēnshì bái máng le._
  - He was busy all day but accomplished nothing; he really busied himself ==in vain==.

- 买一送一，这瓶饮料等于是==白==给的。
  - _Mǎi yī sòng yī, zhè píng yǐnliào děngyú shì bái gěi de._
  - Buy one get one free; this bottle of drink was practically given ==for free==.

- 既然是==白==给的，那我就收下了。
  - _Jìrán shì bái gěi de, nà wǒ jiù shōuxià le._
  - Since it's given ==for free==, then I'll take it.
