# 白 (bái) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-白](../HSK3-19-白.md)
- **Characters**: [白](../characters/白.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我不想==白==拿你的礼物。
  - *Wǒ bùxiǎng bái ná nǐ de lǐwù.*
  - I don't want to take your gift ==for free==.

- 如果你不去考试，你这学期的努力就==白==费了。
  - *Rúguǒ nǐ bú qù kǎoshì, nǐ zhè xuéqī de nǔlì jiù báifèi le.*
  - If you don't take the exam, your efforts this semester will be ==in vain==.

- 那个电影太无聊了，我们简直是==白==看了一场。
  - *Nàge diànyǐng tài wúliáo le, wǒmen jiǎnzhí shì bái kàn le yì chǎng.*
  - That movie was too boring; we basically watched it ==for nothing==.

- 他忙了一整天，结果什么都没做成，真是==白==忙了。
  - *Tā máng le yì zhěng tiān, jiéguǒ shénme dōu méi zuòchéng, zhēnshì bái máng le.*
  - He was busy all day but accomplished nothing; he really busied himself ==in vain==.

- 买一送一，这瓶饮料等于是==白==给的。
  - *Mǎi yī sòng yī, zhè píng yǐnliào děngyú shì bái gěi de.*
  - Buy one get one free; this bottle of drink was practically given ==for free==.

- 你不告诉他地址，他不是==白==来了吗？
  - *Nǐ bú gàosu tā dìzhǐ, tā búshì bái lái le ma?*
  - If you don't tell him the address, didn't he come ==for nothing==?

- 既然是==白==给的，那我就收下了。
  - *Jìrán shì bái gěi de, nà wǒ jiù shōuxià le.*
  - Since it's given ==for free==, then I'll take it.
