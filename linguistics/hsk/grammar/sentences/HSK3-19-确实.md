# 确实 (quèshí) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-确实](../HSK3-19-确实.md)
- **Characters**: [确实](../characters/确实.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 这个问题==确实==很复杂，我们得好好研究一下。
  - *Zhège wèntí quèshí hěn fùzá, wǒmen dé hǎohǎo yánjiū yīxià.*
  - This problem is ==indeed== very complex; we need to study it carefully.

- 他==确实==是一个好人，总是乐于助人。
  - *Tā quèshí shì yígè hǎorén, zǒngshì lèyúzhùrén.*
  - He is ==indeed== a good person, always happy to help others.

- 这家餐厅的价格==确实==有点高，但是味道很好。
  - *Zhèjiā cāntīng de jiàgé quèshí yǒudiǎn gāo, dànshì wèidào hěn hǎo.*
  - The prices at this restaurant are ==indeed== a bit high, but the taste is very good.

- 我今天==确实==没看见他，不知道他去哪儿了。
  - *Wǒ jīntiān quèshí méi kànjiàn tā, bù zhīdào tā qù nǎér le.*
  - I ==indeed== haven't seen him today; I don't know where he went.

- 外面的风很大，天气==确实==非常冷。
  - *Wàimiàn de fēng hěndà, tiānqì quèshí fēicháng lěng.*
  - The wind outside is strong; the weather is ==indeed== very cold.
