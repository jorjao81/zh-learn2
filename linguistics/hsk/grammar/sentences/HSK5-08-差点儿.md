# 差点儿 (chàdiǎnr) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-差点儿](../HSK5-08-差点儿.md)
- **Characters**: [差](../characters/差.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 路太滑了，我==差点儿==摔了一跤。
  - *Lù tài huá le, wǒ ==chàdiǎnr== shuāi le yì jiāo.*
  - The road was too slippery; I ==almost== fell down.

- 今天早上起晚了，==差点儿==没赶上火车。
  - *Jīntiān zǎoshang qǐ wǎn le, ==chàdiǎnr== méi gǎnshàng huǒchē.*
  - I got up late this morning and ==almost== didn't catch the train.

- 那个杯子掉在地上，==差点儿==碎了。
  - *Nàge bēizi diào zài dìshang, ==chàdiǎnr== suì le.*
  - That cup fell on the ground and ==almost== broke.

- 我==差点儿==把这件重要的事情告诉他了。
  - *Wǒ ==chàdiǎnr== bǎ zhè jiàn zhòngyào de shìqing gàosu tā le.*
  - I ==almost== told him this important matter.

- 刚才那辆车开得太快，==差点儿==撞到人。
  - *Gāngcái nà liàng chē kāi de tài kuài, ==chàdiǎnr== zhuàngdào rén.*
  - That car was driving too fast just now and ==almost== hit someone.
