# 等于 (děngyú) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-等于](../NON-HSK-等于.md)
- **Characters**: [等于](../characters/等于.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 一公里==等于==一千米。
  - *Yì gōnglǐ ==děngyú== yìqiān mǐ.*
  - One kilometer ==equals== one thousand meters.

- 你不说话就==等于==默认了。
  - *Nǐ bù shuōhuà jiù ==děngyú== mòrèn le.*
  - Not speaking ==amounts to== tacit acceptance.

- 只学语法不练习，==等于==没学。
  - *Zhǐ xué yǔfǎ bú liànxí, ==děngyú== méi xué.*
  - Learning grammar without practicing ==is just like== not learning at all.

- 在有些文化里，摇头不一定==等于==否定。
  - *Zài yǒuxiē wénhuà lǐ, yáotóu bù yídìng ==děngyú== fǒudìng.*
  - In some cultures, shaking one's head does not necessarily ==equate to== disapproval.

- 迟到二十分钟就==等于==缺席。
  - *Chídào èrshí fēnzhōng jiù ==děngyú== quēxí.*
  - Being twenty minutes late ==is tantamount to== being absent.
