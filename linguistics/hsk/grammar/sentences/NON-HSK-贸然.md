# 贸然 (màorán) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-贸然](../NON-HSK-贸然.md)
- **Characters**: [贸然](../characters/贸然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 在没有调查清楚之前，请不要==贸然==下结论。
  - *Zài méiyǒu diàochá qīngchu zhīqián, qǐng búyào ==màorán== xià jiélùn.*
  - Before investigating thoroughly, please do not ==rashly== draw conclusions.

- 你这样==贸然==辞职，以后生活怎么办？
  - *Nǐ zhèyàng ==màorán== cízhí, yǐhòu shēnghuó zěnmebàn?*
  - If you ==impulsively== quit your job like this, how will you manage your life later?

- 这是一个非常危险的决定，我们不能==贸然==行动。
  - *Zhè shì yí gè fēicháng wēixiǎn de juédìng, wǒmen bùnéng ==màorán== xíngdòng.*
  - This is a very dangerous decision; we cannot act ==blindly==.

- 他不应该==贸然==介入别人的家庭纠纷。
  - *Tā bù yīnggāi ==màorán== jièrù biérén de jiātíng jiūfēn.*
  - He shouldn't have ==presumptuously== got involved in other people's family disputes.

- 还没准备好就==贸然==投资，很容易亏本。
  - *Hái méi zhǔnbèi hǎo jiù ==màorán== tóuzī, hěn róngyì kuīběn.*
  - To ==rashly== invest without being prepared makes it very easy to lose money.
