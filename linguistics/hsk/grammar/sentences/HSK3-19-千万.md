# 千万 (qiānwàn) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-千万](../HSK3-19-千万.md)
- **Characters**: [千万](../characters/千万.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 开车时==千万==不要看手机。
  - _Kāichē shí ==qiānwàn== búyào kàn shǒujī._
  - When driving, you ==absolutely must== not look at your phone.

- 这是一个秘密，你==千万==别告诉别人。
  - _Zhè shì yí gè mìmì, nǐ ==qiānwàn== bié gàosu biérén._
  - This is a secret; you ==must by no means== tell anyone else.

- 这次机会很难得，你==千万==要抓住。
  - _Zhè cì jīhuì hěn nándé, nǐ ==qiānwàn== yào zhuāzhù._
  - This opportunity is hard to come by; you ==by all means== must seize it.

- 明天有重要会议，你==千万==不能迟到。
  - _Míngtiān yǒu zhòngyào huìyì, nǐ ==qiānwàn== bùnéng chídào._
  - There is an important meeting tomorrow; you ==must not== be late.

- 出门在外，==千万==要注意安全。
  - _Chūmén zàiwài, ==qiānwàn== yào zhùyì ānquán._
  - When you are away from home, ==by all means== pay attention to safety.

- 医生嘱咐我，==千万==要按时吃药。
  - _Yīshēng zhǔfù wǒ, ==qiānwàn== yào ànshí chī yào._
  - The doctor told me I ==must== take the medicine on time.
