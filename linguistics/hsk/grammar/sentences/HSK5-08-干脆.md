# 干脆 (gāncuì) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-干脆](../HSK5-08-干脆.md)
- **Characters**: [干脆](../characters/干脆.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 既然修不好了，==干脆==买个新的吧。
  - *Jìrán xiū bù hǎo le, ==gāncuì== mǎi gè xīn de ba.*
  - Since it can't be fixed, let's ==just== buy a new one.

- 别做饭了，我们==干脆==叫外卖吧。
  - *Bié zuòfàn le, wǒmen ==gāncuì== jiào wàimài ba.*
  - Don't cook; let's ==simply== order takeout.

- 他觉得这里太吵，==干脆==搬到了乡下住。
  - *Tā juéde zhèlǐ tài chǎo, ==gāncuì== bāndào le xiāngxià zhù.*
  - He felt it was too noisy here, so he ==simply== moved to the countryside.

- 他的回答非常==干脆==，一点儿也不犹豫。
  - *Tā de huídá fēicháng ==gāncuì==, yìdiǎnr yě bù yóuyù.*
  - His answer was very ==straightforward==, without any hesitation.

- 如果你不想去，就==干脆==告诉他，别让他一直等你。
  - *Rúguǒ nǐ bù xiǎng qù, jiù ==gāncuì== gàosu tā, bié ràng tā yìzhí děng nǐ.*
  - If you don't want to go, ==just== tell him; don't let him keep waiting for you.
