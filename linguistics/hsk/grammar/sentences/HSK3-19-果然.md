# 果然 (guǒrán) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-果然](../HSK3-19-果然.md)
- **Characters**: [果然](../characters/果然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 他说今天会来，下午==果然==来了。
  - *Tā shuō jīntiān huì lái, xiàwǔ guǒrán lái le.*
  - He said he would come today, and in the afternoon he ==sure enough== came.

- 医生说打针会有点疼，==果然==很疼。
  - *Yīshēng shuō dǎzhēn huì yǒudiǎn téng, guǒrán hěn téng.*
  - The doctor said the injection would hurt a little, and it ==sure enough== hurt a lot.

- 便宜没好货，那个便宜的包==果然==很快就坏了。
  - *Piányi méihǎo huò, nàge piányi de bāo guǒrán hěnkuài jiù huài le.*
  - Cheap goods are not good; that cheap bag ==as expected== broke very quickly.

- 我猜你在书房，进去一看，你==果然==在。
  - *Wǒ cāi nǐ zài shūfáng, jìnqù yīkàn, nǐ guǒrán zài.*
  - I guessed you were in the study; I went in to look, and ==sure enough== you were there.

- 天气预报说今天有雪，早上==果然==下雪了。
  - *Tiānqìyùbào shuō jīntiān yǒuxuě, zǎoshàng guǒrán xiàxuě le.*
  - The weather forecast said there would be snow today, and this morning it ==as expected== snowed.
