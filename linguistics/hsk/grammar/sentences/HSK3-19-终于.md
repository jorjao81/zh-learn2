# 终于 (zhōngyú) — HSK3-19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-终于](../HSK3-19-终于.md)
- **Characters**: [终于](../characters/终于.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 火车==终于==到了，我们可以回家了。
  - *Huǒchē zhōngyú dào le, wǒmen kěyǐ huíjiā le.*
  - The train ==finally== arrived; we can go home.

- 我们讨论了很久，==终于==找到了解决问题的办法。
  - *Wǒmen tǎolùn le hěn jiǔ, zhōngyú zhǎodào le jiějuéwèntí de bànfǎ.*
  - We discussed for a long time and ==finally== found a way to solve the problem.

- 下了一个星期的雨，太阳==终于==出来了。
  - *Xià le yígè xīngqī de yǔ, tàiyáng zhōngyú chūlái le.*
  - It rained for a week, and the sun ==finally== came out.

- 面对证据，他==终于==承认了错误。
  - *Miànduì zhèngjù, tā zhōngyú chéngrèn le cuòwù.*
  - Faced with the evidence, he ==finally== admitted his mistake.

- 我解释了好几遍，你==终于==明白了。
  - *Wǒ jiěshì le hǎojǐbiàn, nǐ zhōngyú míngbái le.*
  - I explained it several times; you ==finally== understood.
