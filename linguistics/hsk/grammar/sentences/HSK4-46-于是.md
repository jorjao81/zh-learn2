# 于是 (yúshì) — 四46

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-46-于是](../HSK4-46-于是.md)
- **Characters**: [于是](../characters/于是.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 听说那里打折，==于是==她买了很多衣服。
  - *Tīngshuō nàlǐ dǎzhé, ==yúshì== tā mǎi le hěn duō yīfu.*
  - Hearing that there was a discount, ==so== she bought a lot of clothes.

- 我找不到路了，==于是==向路边的警察询问。
  - *Wǒ zhǎo bú dào lù le, ==yúshì== xiàng lùbiān de jǐngchá xúnwèn.*
  - I couldn't find the way, ==thereupon== I asked the police officer on the roadside.

- 看到大家都站起来鼓掌，==于是==他也站了起来。
  - *Kàndào dàjiā dōu zhàn qǐlái gǔzhǎng, ==yúshì== tā yě zhàn le qǐlái.*
  - Seeing everyone stand up and applaud, ==so== he stood up too.

- 这道题太难了，==于是==我放弃了。
  - *Zhè dào tí tài nán le, ==yúshì== wǒ fàngqì le.*
  - This question was too hard, ==so== I gave up.

- 他发现钱包不见了，==于是==马上报了警。
  - *Tā fāxiàn qiánbāo bújiàn le, ==yúshì== mǎshàng bào le jǐng.*
  - He found his wallet missing, ==and thereupon== immediately reported it to the police.
