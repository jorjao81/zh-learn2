# 到底 (dàodǐ) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-到底](../HSK3-19-到底.md)
- **Characters**: [到底](../characters/到底.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 你说了半天，==到底==想表达什么？
  - *Nǐ shuō le bàntiān, dàodǐ xiǎng biǎodá shénme?*
  - You've been talking for ages; what ==on earth== do you want to express?

- 这本书我找了好久，今天==到底==买到了。
  - *Zhè běn shū wǒ zhǎo le hǎojiǔ, jīntiān dàodǐ mǎi dào le.*
  - I looked for this book for a long time, and today I ==finally== bought it.

- 北京==到底==是首都，交通非常方便。
  - *Běijīng dàodǐ shì shǒudū, jiāotōng fēicháng fāngbiàn.*
  - Beijing is, ==after all==, the capital; the transportation is very convenient.

- 遇到困难不要放弃，我们要坚持==到底==。
  - *Yùdào kùnnán búyào fàngqì, wǒmen yào jiānchí dàodǐ.*
  - When encountering difficulties, don't give up; we must persist ==to the end==.

- 那个人==到底==是谁？我觉得很面熟。
  - *Nàge rén dàodǐ shì shéi? Wǒ juéde hěn miànshú.*
  - Who ==on earth== is that person? I feel he looks very familiar.

- 你==到底==选哪一个？快决定吧。
  - *Nǐ dàodǐ xuǎn nǎ yí gè? Kuài juédìng ba.*
  - Which one are you choosing ==after all==? Decide quickly.

- 谁==到底==是这件事的负责人？
  - *Shéi dàodǐ shì zhè jiàn shì de fùzérén?*
  - Who ==on earth== is the person in charge of this matter?
