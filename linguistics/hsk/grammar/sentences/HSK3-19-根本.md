# 根本 (gēnběn) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-根本](../HSK3-19-根本.md)
- **Characters**: [根本](../characters/根本.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 他==根本==不听我的解释，直接走了。
  - *Tā gēnběn bù tīng wǒ de jiěshì, zhíjiē zǒu le.*
  - He didn't listen to my explanation ==at all==; he just left.

- 这个计划的==根本==目的是提高效率。
  - *Zhège jìhuà de gēnběn mùdì shì tígāo xiàolǜ.*
  - The ==fundamental== purpose of this plan is to improve efficiency.

- 我以前==根本==不知道这件事。
  - *Wǒ yǐqián gēnběn bù zhīdào zhè jiàn shì.*
  - I didn't know about this matter ==at all== before.

- 要想学好汉语，==根本==在于多练习。
  - *Yào xiǎng xué hǎo Hànyǔ, gēnběn zàiyú duō liànxí.*
  - If you want to learn Chinese well, the ==root== (fundamental key) lies in practicing more.

- 对于这个决定，他==根本==不在乎。
  - *Duìyú zhège juédìng, tā gēnběn bú zàihu.*
  - Regarding this decision, he doesn't care ==at all==.

- 他==根本==没把我的话当回事。
  - *Tā gēnběn méi bǎ wǒ de huà dāng huí shì.*
  - He didn't take my words seriously ==at all==.

- 解决贫困才是==根本==。
  - *Jiějué pínkùn cái shì gēnběn.*
  - Solving poverty is the ==root==.
