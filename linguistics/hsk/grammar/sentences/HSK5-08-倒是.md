# 倒是 (dàoshì) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-倒是](../HSK5-08-倒是.md)
- **Characters**: [倒是](../characters/倒是.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 房间小==倒是==小，不过很干净。
  - *Fángjiān xiǎo ==dàoshì== xiǎo, búguò hěn gānjìng.*
  - The room ==is indeed== small, but it is very clean.

- 我以为今天会下雨，天气==倒是==挺不错的。
  - *Wǒ yǐwéi jīntiān huì xiàyǔ, tiānqì ==dàoshì== tǐng búcuò de.*
  - I thought it would rain today, but the weather is ==actually== quite nice.

- 他不着急，你==倒是==急起来了。
  - *Tā bù zháojí, nǐ ==dàoshì== jí qǐlái le.*
  - He isn't anxious, yet you ==actually== started getting anxious.

- 这个办法==倒是==可以试一试。
  - *Zhège bànfǎ ==dàoshì== kěyǐ shìyíshì.*
  - This method ==actually== might be worth a try.

- 我平时不爱吃辣，但这道菜==倒是==很合我的口味。
  - *Wǒ píngshí bú ài chī là, dàn zhè dào cài ==dàoshì== hěn hé wǒ de kǒuwèi.*
  - I usually don't like spicy food, but this dish actually ==suits== my taste well.
