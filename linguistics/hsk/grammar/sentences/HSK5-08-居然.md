# 居然 (jūrán) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-居然](../HSK5-08-居然.md)
- **Characters**: [居然](../characters/居然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 都四月了，外面==居然==下雪了！
  - *Dōu sìyuè le, wàimiàn ==jūrán== xiàxuě le!*
  - It's already April, and it is ==actually== snowing outside!

- 这么小的小孩==居然==会弹钢琴，真让人吃惊。
  - *Zhème xiǎo de xiǎohái ==jūrán== huì tán gāngqín, zhēn ràng rén chījīng.*
  - Such a small child can ==actually== play the piano; it's really surprising.

- 我忙得==居然==忘了今天是我的生日。
  - *Wǒ máng de ==jūrán== wàngle jīntiān shì wǒ de shēngrì.*
  - I was so busy that I ==actually== forgot today was my birthday.

- 在国外旅游的时候，我==居然==遇到了我的小学同学。
  - *Zài guówài lǚyóu de shíhou, wǒ ==jūrán== yùdào le wǒ de xiǎoxué tóngxué.*
  - While traveling abroad, I ==unexpectedly== ran into my elementary school classmate.

- 他开会的时候==居然==睡着了。
  - *Tā kāihuì de shíhou ==jūrán== shuìzháo le.*
  - He ==actually== fell asleep during the meeting.
