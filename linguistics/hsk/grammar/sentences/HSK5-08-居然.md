# 居然 (jūrán) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-居然](../HSK5-08-居然.md)
- **Characters**: [居然](../characters/居然.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 没想到平时不爱运动的他，==居然==跑了第一名。
  - *Méi xiǎngdào píngshí bú ài yùndòng de tā, ==jūrán== pǎo le dì-yī míng.*
  - I didn't expect that he, who usually doesn't like sports, ==actually== ran first place.

- 这本书这么厚，你==居然==一天就看完了。
  - *Zhè běn shū zhème hòu, nǐ ==jūrán== yì tiān jiù kàn wán le.*
  - This book is so thick, and you ==to my surprise== finished reading it in one day.

- 他==居然==忘了今天是妻子的生日。
  - *Tā ==jūrán== wàng le jīntiān shì qīzi de shēngrì.*
  - He ==actually== forgot that today is his wife's birthday.

- 这里的冬天==居然==比夏天还热，真是太奇怪了。
  - *Zhèlǐ de dōngtiān ==jūrán== bǐ xiàtiān hái rè, zhēnshi tài qíguài le.*
  - The winter here is ==unexpectedly== hotter than the summer; it's really too strange.

- 找了半天，眼镜==居然==就在我鼻子上。
  - *Zhǎo le bàntiān, yǎnjìng ==jūrán== jiù zài wǒ bízi shang.*
  - I looked for a long time, and the glasses were ==actually== right on my nose.
