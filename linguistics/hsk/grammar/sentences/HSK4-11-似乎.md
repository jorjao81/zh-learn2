# 似乎 (sìhū) — 四11

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-11-似乎](../HSK4-11-似乎.md)
- **Characters**: [似乎](../characters/似乎.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 天==似乎==要下雨了。
  - *Tiān sìhū yào xiàyǔ le.*
  - It ==seems like== it is going to rain.

- 他==似乎==不明白我的意思。
  - *Tā sìhū bù míngbai wǒ de yìsi.*
  - He ==seems== not to understand what I mean.

- 这篇文章的观点==似乎==有些偏激。
  - *Zhè piān wénzhāng de guāndiǎn sìhū yǒuxiē piānjī.*
  - The viewpoint of this article ==seems== a bit extreme.

- 一夜之间，春天==似乎==已经到了。
  - *Yīyè zhī jiān, chūntiān sìhū yǐjīng dào le.*
  - Overnight, it ==seems that== spring has already arrived.

- 他看着我，==似乎==有很多话要说似的。
  - *Tā kànzhe wǒ, sìhū yǒu hěnduō huà yào shuō shìde.*
  - He looked at me ==as if== he had a lot to say.
