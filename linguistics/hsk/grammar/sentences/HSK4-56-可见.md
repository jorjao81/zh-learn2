# 可见 (kějiàn) — 四56

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-56-可见](../HSK4-56-可见.md)
- **Characters**: [可见](../characters/可见.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 这么晚了他还在工作，==可见==他很敬业。
  - *Zhème wǎn le tā hái zài gōngzuò, kějiàn tā hěn jìngyè.*
  - He is still working this late; ==it is clear that== he is very dedicated.

- 这里的街道非常干净，==可见==城市管理得很好。
  - *Zhèlǐ de jiēdào fēicháng gānjìng, kějiàn chéngshì guǎnlǐ de hěn hǎo.*
  - The streets here are very clean; ==it can be seen that== the city is managed well.

- 他能把这么难的题做出来，==可见==他非常聪明。
  - *Tā néng bǎ zhème nán de tí zuò chūlai, kějiàn tā fēicháng cōngming.*
  - He can solve such a difficult problem; ==this shows that== he is very smart.

- 超市里的人这么多，==可见==大家都开始准备过年了。
  - *Chāoshì lǐ de rén zhème duō, kějiàn dàjiā dōu kāishǐ zhǔnbèi guònián le.*
  - There are so many people in the supermarket; ==it is obvious that== everyone has started preparing for the New Year.

- 这本书再版了十次，==可见==它非常受欢迎。
  - *Zhè běn shū zàibǎn le shí cì, kějiàn tā fēicháng shòu huānyíng.*
  - This book has been reprinted ten times; ==it is clear that== it is very popular.
