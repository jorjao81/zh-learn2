# 老 (lǎo) — 二16

- **Grammar**: [HSK2-16-老](../HSK2-16-老.md)
- **Characters**: [老](../characters/老.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我这两天**老**丢东西。
  - *Wǒ zhè liǎng tiān **lǎo** diū dōngxi.*
  - I keep **always** losing things these past couple of days.

- 那个小孩儿**老**哭，真烦人。
  - *Nàge xiǎoháir **lǎo** kū, zhēn fánrén.*
  - That kid **keeps** crying; it's so annoying.

- 你别**老**看电视，去复习一下。
  - *Nǐ bié **lǎo** kàn diànshì, qù fùxí yíxià.*
  - Don't **always** watch TV; go review a bit.

- 为什么我**老**觉得饿？
  - *Wèishéme wǒ **lǎo** juéde è?*
  - Why do I **constantly** feel hungry?

- 这台电脑**老**出问题。
  - *Zhè tái diànnǎo **lǎo** chū wèntí.*
  - This computer **always** has problems.
