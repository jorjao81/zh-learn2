# 索性 (suǒxìng) — 七16

- **Grammar**: [HSK7-9-16-索性](../HSK7-9-16-索性.md)
- **Characters**: [索性](../characters/索性.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 等了一个小时车还没来，我==索性==走回家了。
  - *Děng le yí gè xiǎoshí chē hái méi lái, wǒ ==suǒxìng== zǒu huí jiā le.*
  - The bus didn't come after waiting for an hour, so I ==just== walked home.

- 既然大家都不想去，那我们==索性==取消这次聚会吧。
  - *Jìrán dàjiā dōu bù xiǎng qù, nà wǒmen ==suǒxìng== qǔxiāo zhè cì jùhuì ba.*
  - Since no one wants to go, let's ==might as well== cancel this gathering.

- 这台旧电脑太慢了，他==索性==把它扔了。
  - *Zhè tái jiù diànnǎo tài màn le, tā ==suǒxìng== bǎ tā rēng le.*
  - This old computer was too slow, so he ==just went ahead and== threw it away.

- 与其即使去了也迟到，不如==索性==请假在家休息。
  - *Yǔqí jíshǐ qù le yě chídào, bùrú ==suǒxìng== qǐngjià zài jiā xiūxi.*
  - Rather than being late even if I go, it's better to ==just== take leave and rest at home.

- 不管是修还是买新的都很麻烦，我==索性==不用了。
  - *Bùguǎn shì xiū háishì mǎi xīn de dōu hěn máfan, wǒ ==suǒxìng== bú yòng le.*
  - Whether repairing or buying a new one is a hassle, so I ==just== won't use it anymore.
