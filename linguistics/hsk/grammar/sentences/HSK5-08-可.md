# 可 (kě) — 五08

- **Grammar**: [HSK5-08-可](../HSK5-08-可.md)
- **Characters**: [可](../characters/可.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我==可==没答应过这种事，你别乱说。
  - *Wǒ ==kě== méi dāying guò zhè zhǒng shì, nǐ bié luàn shuō.*
  - I ==certainly== never agreed to such a thing; don't speak nonsense.

- 你一个人出门在外，==可==要自己照顾好自己。
  - *Nǐ yí gè rén chūmén zàiwài, ==kě== yào zìjǐ zhàogù hǎo zìjǐ.*
  - Being away from home alone, you ==really== must take good care of yourself.

- 这次考试非常重要，你==可==千万别大意。
  - *Zhè cì kǎoshì fēicháng zhòngyào, nǐ ==kě== qiānwàn bié dàyì.*
  - This exam is very important; you ==absolutely== must not be careless.

- 这么贵的手机丢了，==可==心疼死我了。
  - *Zhème guì de shǒujī diū le, ==kě== xīnténg sǐ wǒ le.*
  - Losing such an expensive phone, I am ==really== heartbroken.

- 他要是知道真相，那==可==不得了。
  - *Tā yàoshi zhīdào zhēnxiàng, nà ==kě== bùdéliǎo.*
  - If he finds out the truth, that would ==really== be a disaster.
