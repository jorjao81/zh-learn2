# 果断 (guǒduàn) — NON-HSK

- **Grammar**: [NON-HSK-果断](../NON-HSK-果断.md)
- **Characters**: [果断](../characters/果断.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 机会来了就要==果断==抓住。
  - *Jīhuì lái le jiù yào ==guǒduàn== zhuāzhù.*
  - When the opportunity comes, you must seize it ==decisively==.

- 他==果断==地拒绝了这个不合理的要求。
  - *Tā ==guǒduàn== de jùjué le zhè ge bù hélǐ de yāoqiú.*
  - He ==firmly== rejected this unreasonable request.

- 在这种紧急情况下，必须==果断==行动。
  - *Zài zhè zhǒng jǐnjí qíngkuàng xià, bìxū ==guǒduàn== xíngdòng.*
  - In this kind of emergency situation, one must act ==decisively==.

- 她性格很==果断==，从来不拖泥带水。
  - *Tā xìnggé hěn ==guǒduàn==, cónglái bù tuōnídàishuǐ.*
  - She has a very ==decisive== character and never drags things out.

- 为了公司的未来，经理==果断==放弃了这个项目。
  - *Wèile gōngsī de wèilái, jīnglǐ ==guǒduàn== fàngqì le zhè ge xiàngmù.*
  - For the future of the company, the manager ==decisively== gave up on this project.
