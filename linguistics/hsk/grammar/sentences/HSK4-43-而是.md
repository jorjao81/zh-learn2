# 而是 (érshì) — 四43

- **Grammar**: [HSK4-43-而是](../HSK4-43-而是.md)
- **Characters**: [而是](../characters/而是.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我没哭，==而是==沙子进眼睛了。
  - *Wǒ méi kū, érshì shāzi jìn yǎnjīng le.*
  - I didn't cry, ==but rather== sand got in my eyes.

- 成功的关键不是运气，==而是==坚持。
  - *Chénggōng de guānjiàn búshì yùnqi, érshì jiānchí.*
  - The key to success is not luck, ==but== persistence.

- 他不是去旅游，==而是==去出差。
  - *Tā búshì qù lǚyóu, érshì qù chūchāi.*
  - He is not going for tourism, ==but rather== for a business trip.

- 问题不是出在电脑上，==而是==出在软件上。
  - *Wèntí búshì chū zài diànnǎo shang, érshì chū zài ruǎnjiàn shang.*
  - The problem is not with the computer, ==but rather== with the software.

- 这一天不是结束，==而是==开始。
  - *Zhè yī tiān búshì jiéshù, érshì kāishǐ.*
  - This day is not an end, ==but rather== a beginning.
