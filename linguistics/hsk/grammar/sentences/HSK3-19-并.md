# 并 (bìng) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-并](../HSK3-19-并.md)
- **Characters**: [并](../characters/并.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我==并==不想打扰你，只是想问个问题。
  - _Wǒ ==bìng== bùxiǎng dǎrǎo nǐ, zhǐshì xiǎng wèn gè wèntí._
  - I ==actually== don't want to disturb you; I just want to ask a question.

- 这道菜看着很辣，但==并==不辣。
  - _Zhè dào cài kànzhe hěn là, dàn ==bìng== bú là._
  - This dish looks very spicy, but it's ==actually not== spicy.

- 我们公司已经开发==并==测试了这个新软件。
  - _Wǒmen gōngsī yǐjīng kāifā ==bìng== cèshì le zhège xīn ruǎnjiàn._
  - Our company has already developed ==and== tested this new software.

- 他说他会来，但他==并==没有出现。
  - _Tā shuō tā huì lái, dàn tā ==bìng== méiyǒu chūxiàn._
  - He said he would come, but he ==actually== didn't show up.

- 医生检查了病人，==并==开了一些药。
  - _Yīshēng jiǎnchá le bìngrén, ==bìng== kāi le yìxiē yào._
  - The doctor examined the patient ==and== prescribed some medicine.

- 他虽然说没时间，但==并==不是真的忙。
  - _Tā suīrán shuō méi shíjiān, dàn ==bìng== búshì zhēnde máng._
  - Although he said he has no time, he's ==actually== not really busy.

- 我==并==不完全同意你的看法。
  - _Wǒ ==bìng== bù wánquán tóngyì nǐ de kànfǎ._
  - I don't ==actually== completely agree with your view.

- 这部电影==并==不像大家说的那么无聊。
  - _Zhè bù diànyǐng ==bìng== bú xiàng dàjiā shuō de nàme wúliáo._
  - This movie isn't ==actually== as boring as everyone says.

- 事情的真相==并==不是你想的那样。
  - _Shìqing de zhēnxiàng ==bìng== bú shì nǐ xiǎng de nàyàng._
  - The truth of the matter is ==really== not what you think.

- 我==并==不想打扰你休息。
  - _Wǒ ==bìng== bù xiǎng dǎrǎo nǐ xiūxi._
  - I ==really== don't want to disturb your rest.

- 那个地方我虽然去过，但==并==不熟悉。
  - _Nàge dìfang wǒ suīrán qù guò, dàn ==bìng== bù shúxī._
  - Although I have been to that place, I am ==actually== not familiar with it.
