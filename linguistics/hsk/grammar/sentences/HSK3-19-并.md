# 并 (bìng) — 三19

- **Grammar**: [HSK3-19-并](../HSK3-19-并.md)
- **Characters**: [并](../characters/并.md)

## Sentence Exercises

- 我==并==不想打扰你，只是想问个问题。
  - *Wǒ bìng bùxiǎng dǎrǎo nǐ, zhǐshì xiǎng wèn gè wèntí.*
  - I ==actually== don't want to disturb you; I just want to ask a question.

- 这道菜看着很辣，但==并==不辣。
  - *Zhè dào cài kànzhe hěn là, dàn bìng bú là.*
  - This dish looks very spicy, but it's ==actually not== spicy.

- 我们公司已经开发==并==测试了这个新软件。
  - *Wǒmen gōngsī yǐjīng kāifā bìng cèshì le zhège xīn ruǎnjiàn.*
  - Our company has already developed ==and== tested this new software.

- 他说他会来，但他==并==没有出现。
  - *Tā shuō tā huì lái, dàn tā bìng méiyǒu chūxiàn.*
  - He said he would come, but he ==actually== didn't show up.

- 医生检查了病人，==并==开了一些药。
  - *Yīshēng jiǎnchá le bìngrén, bìng kāi le yìxiē yào.*
  - The doctor examined the patient ==and== prescribed some medicine.

- 他虽然说没时间，但==并==不是真的忙。
  - *Tā suīrán shuō méi shíjiān, dàn bìng búshì zhēnde máng.*
  - Although he said he has no time, he's ==actually== not really busy.

- 请核对信息==并==确认。
  - *Qǐng héduì xìnxī bìng quèrèn.*
  - Please verify the information ==and== confirm.
