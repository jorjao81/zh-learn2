### A.4.2 Phrases (短语)

#### A.4.2.1 Fixed Phrases (固定短语)

##### A.4.2.1.1 Four-Character Patterns (四字格)

| # | Pattern | Structure | Examples |
|---|---------|-----------|----------|
| 四23 | 大A大B | 大 + monosyllable + 大 + monosyllable (emphatic intensification) | 你这大吃大喝的毛病对身体不好，一定要改改。*Your habit of eating and drinking excessively is bad for your health, you must change it.* / 她心情不好，为一点儿小事就大吵大闹。*She's in a bad mood, making a big fuss over small things.* |

##### A.4.2.1.2 Other Patterns (其他)

| # | Pattern | Chinese | Pinyin | Examples |
|---|---------|---------|--------|----------|
| 四25 | It seems/appears | 看来 | kànlái | 看来他是个好人。*It seems he's a good person.* / 看来明天不会再下雨了。*It looks like it won't rain again tomorrow.* / 看来这次考试他能通过。*It appears he can pass this exam.* |
| 四26 | Have/not have enough time | 来得及/来不及 | láidejí/láibují | 你别着急，时间来得及。*Don't worry, there's enough time.* / 现在刚六点，你马上去还来得及。*It's only six o'clock now, you can still make it if you go right away.* / 来不及了，我们快走吧。*There's no time left, let's go quickly.* / 时间还早，不会来不及的。*It's still early, we won't run out of time.* |

| 四28 | Generally speaking | 一般来说 | yìbān lái shuō | 一般来说，选手参加了比赛是不能退出的。*Generally speaking, once athletes enter a competition they cannot withdraw.* / 一般来说，这么重要的场合他是不会迟到的。*Generally speaking, he wouldn't be late for such an important occasion.* / 一般来说，跟青年人相比，老年人的经验更丰富。*Generally speaking, compared to young people, older people have more experience.* |

### A.4.3 Fixed Patterns (固定格式)

| # | Pattern | Structure | Examples |
|---|---------|-----------|----------|
| 四29 | Each one more... than the last | 一 + MW + 比 + 一 + MW | 这些球鞋一双比一双好看。*Each pair of sneakers looks better than the last.* / 他的演出一次比一次精彩。*Each of his performances is more spectacular than the last.* / 天气一天比一天暖和。*The weather gets warmer day by day.* |
| 四30 | Since (a point in time) | (自) + Time + 以来 | 自去年以来，我一直生活在北京。*Since last year, I've been living in Beijing.* / 上大学以来，他一直坚持学习中文。*Since starting university, he has been persistently studying Chinese.* / 自有了孩子以来，她每天都很忙。*Since having a child, she's been busy every day.* |
| 四31 | A is composed of B | A + 由 + B + 组成 | 我们班由两位老师和二十位学生组成。*Our class is composed of two teachers and twenty students.* / 这篇文章由三个部分组成。*This article consists of three parts.* / 这张试卷是由十道选择题和一道写作题组成的。*This test paper is composed of ten multiple choice questions and one essay question.* |
| 四32 | In terms of/Regarding | 在 + N + 方面 | 在这方面，我没有什么经验。*In this regard, I don't have much experience.* / 在历史方面，他知道得很多。*In terms of history, he knows a lot.* / 在修理电脑方面，她是个专家。*When it comes to computer repair, she's an expert.* |
| 四33 | In/Under/During | 在 + N + 上/下/中 | 在这件事情上，最好多听听父母的意见。*On this matter, it's best to listen more to your parents' opinions.* / 在他的影响下，我喜欢上了音乐。*Under his influence, I came to love music.* / 在这篇课文中，我们一共学了三十个生词。*In this text, we learned a total of thirty new words.* |

### A.4.5 Sentence Types (句子的类型)

#### A.4.5.1 Special Sentence Patterns (特殊句型)

## Proficiency Description

According to the official standard, learners at HSK Level 4 can:

- Use Chinese to communicate on relatively complex topics
- Express themselves fairly standardly and appropriately (表达较为规范、得体)
- Read and understand materials within 500 characters
- Reading speed: no less than 140 characters per minute
- Understand general compound sentences
- Write simple narrative, expository, and practical texts
