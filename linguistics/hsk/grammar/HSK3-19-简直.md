# Grammar Point: 简直 (jiǎnzhí)

| Category | Level | Character | Pinyin | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mood Adverb | HSK 3 (三19) | 简直 | jiǎnzhí | simply; virtually; practically; almost |

## The Core Concept: "Simply Straight" -> Exaggeration

**简直** (jiǎnzhí) is the hallmark of dramatic expression. It breaks down into **简** (simple) + **直** (straight/direct).

It implies: "To put it simply and directly..." or "It is practically..."

It is used to emphasize a state or action that is so extreme it seems unbelievable, or to assert that two different things are essentially the same. It often appears in sentences with an **exaggerated** tone.

**Structure:** `Subject + 简直 (+ 是) + Extreme State / Metaphor`

## 1. Emphasizing Extreme Qualities

When describing an adjective, **简直** acts like a super-charged "very" or "completely," often implying disbelief.

* **这里的风景简直太美了！**
  * *Zhèlǐ de fēngjǐng jiǎnzhí tài měi le!*
  * The scenery here is **simply** too beautiful!
* **听到这个消息，我简直不敢相信自己的耳朵。**
  * *Tīngdào zhège xiāoxi, wǒ jiǎnzhí bùgǎn xiāngxìn zìjǐ de ěrduo.*
  * Hearing this news, I **virtually** couldn't believe my own ears.

## 2. Metaphorical Equivalence ("It's practically...")

This is where **简直** shines. It equates reality with a metaphor. You almost always use **是** (shì) or **像** (xiàng) here.

* **热死我了，今天简直是大火炉。**
  * *Rè sǐ wǒ le, jīntiān jiǎnzhí shì dà huǒlú.*
  * I'm dying of heat; today is **practically** a giant furnace.
* **他简直是个天才。**
  * *Tā jiǎnzhí shì ge tiāncái.*
  * He is **simply** a genius.
* **这简直是开玩笑！**
  * *Zhè jiǎnzhí shì kāi wánxiào!*
  * This is **nothing short of** a joke!

## 3. "Simply" Unable to Do Something

Used with negatives to express total inability or impossibility.

* **这道菜太辣了，我简直吃不下去。**
  * *Zhè dào cài tài là le, wǒ jiǎnzhí chī bú xiàqù.*
  * This dish is too spicy; I **simply** cannot eat it.

## Comparison: 简直 (jiǎnzhí) vs. 几乎 (jīhū)

Both translate to "almost" or "practically," but they differ in **tone**.

| Word | Tone | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **几乎** (jīhū) | Objective | "Nearly" or "Almost." Describes a fact. |
| **简直** (jiǎnzhí) | Subjective/Emotional | "Simply" or "Virtually." Used for exaggeration or complaint. |

* **几乎**没变。 (Almost didn't change.) -> Fact: 99% the same.
* **简直**没变。 (Simply didn't change.) -> Feeling: It's unbelievable how little it changed!

## Collocations

* **简直是**... (Simply is...)
* **简直太**... (Simply too...)
* **简直无法**... (Simply unable to...)
