# Character: 到底 (dàodǐ)

## Compound Breakdown: Arrive + Bottom

### 1. 到 (dào) - To Arrive / To

* **Meaning:** To arrive, to go to, to reach.
* **Direction:** Indicates movement towards a destination or state.

### 2. 底 (dǐ) - Bottom / Base

* **Visual:** Under a shelter (广), something low (氐).
* **Meaning:** The bottom of an object, the base, the end of a period (e.g., end of the year 年底).
* **Common Words:**
  * **海底** (hǎidǐ) - Seabed.
  * **年底** (niándǐ) - End of year.
  * **彻底** (chèdǐ) - Thorough (penetrate to the bottom).

### The Combination: 到底 (dàodǐ)

Literally "Arrive at the bottom."

The transition from spatial to abstract meaning is logical:

1. **Spatial:** Going to the bottom (of a hole/sea).
2. **Temporal:** Going to the end (persist to the end).
3. **Inquisitive:** Digging to the root of a question (What *on earth* is the answer?).
4. **Logical:** Reaching the fundamental truth (He is a child *at the bottom* / after all).
