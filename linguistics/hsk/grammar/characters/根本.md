# Character: 根本 (gēnběn)

## Compound Breakdown: Root + Stem

### 1. 根 (gēn) - Root

* **Radical:** 木 (Tree/Wood).
* **Meaning:** The root of a plant.
* **Extensions:**
  * Root / Source / Origin.
  * Measure word for long, thin objects (like sticks, hairs, cigarettes).
  * **树根** (shùgēn) - Tree root.
  * **根据** (gēnjù) - According to (Based on the root/foundation).

### 2. 本 (běn) - Stem / Root / Book

* **Radical:** 木 (Tree) with a line indicating the base/root.
* **Meaning:**
  * Root / Stem.
  * Foundation / Basis.
  * Book (originally bamboo slips bound together).
  * Measure word for books.
  * **本来** (běnlái) - Originally.
  * **日本** (Rìběn) - Japan (Root of the Sun = Land of the Rising Sun).

### The Combination: 根本 (gēnběn)

Literally "Root + Root/Stem."
It reinforces the idea of the absolute foundation.

* If you say "I **根本** don't know," you are saying "From the very foundation/root, I do not know." It eradicates the possibility completely.
