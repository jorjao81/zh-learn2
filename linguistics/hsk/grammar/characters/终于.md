**终** (zhōng): end / finish / death
- **Etymology**: Silk radical (纟) + Winter (冬). Winter is the end of the seasons/year; the silk knot marks the end of the thread.
- **Meaning**: The termination point. **终点** (destination/terminus).

**于** (yú): at / on / in / to
- **Function**: Preposition.
- **Combination**: **终于** literally means "At the end." It emphasizes reaching the termination point of a timeline.
