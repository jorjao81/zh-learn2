# Character: 当然 (dāngrán)

## Compound Breakdown: Ought To + Thus

### 1. 当 (dāng) - To Match / Ought To
* **Original Meaning:** To match or be equal to.
* **Extensions:**
  * To be in the position of (assume the role): **当老师** (be a teacher).
  * To match the time/place: **当时** (at that time).
  * Ought to / Should: **应当** (ought to).
  * Here, in **当然**, it leans on the "ought to/should" meaning.

### 2. 然 (rán) - Thus / So / Correct
* **Original Meaning:** To burn (visual: dog meat 肽 + fire 灬). This meaning moved to the character **燃** (rán).
* **Grammatical Meaning:** It became a suffix meaning "like this," "state of," or "correct."
* **Common Words:**
  * **忽然** (hūrán) = Suddenly (Sudden-like).
  * **自然** (zìrán) = Nature / Natural (Self-so / Self-existing).
  * **然后** (ránhòu) = Then (Thus-after).

### The Combination: 当然 (dāngrán)
Literally "Should be thus."
* If something "should be so," it is **natural**, **expected**, and a matter of **course**.
