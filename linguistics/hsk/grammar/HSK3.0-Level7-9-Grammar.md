## Proficiency Description

According to the official standard, learners at HSK Levels 7-9 can:

- **Level 7**: Understand and use Chinese in most situations with relative accuracy and fluency; handle complex linguistic tasks in work, study, and social settings
- **Level 8**: Understand and use Chinese in virtually all situations with high accuracy and fluency; conduct professional-level communication and academic discussion
- **Level 9**: Use Chinese like a native speaker; demonstrate near-native proficiency in all contexts including academic, professional, and creative expression
