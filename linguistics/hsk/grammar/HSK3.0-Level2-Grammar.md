| 二55 | 有-Sentence Type 2 | "有"字句2：**(1) Expressing evaluation/attainment (表示评价、达到):** 他有一米八高。*He's 1.8 meters tall.* / 他有三十多岁。*He's in his thirties.* **(2) Expressing comparison:** see【二58】"比较句2-(4)" |
| 二56 | Existential Sentence Type 1 | 存现句1：表示存在：**(1) Place + 有 + Num-MW Phrase + Noun:** see【一37】"'有'字句1-(2)" **(2) Place + Verb + 着 (+ Num-MW Phrase) + Noun:** 桌子上放着一本词典。*A dictionary is placed on the table.* / 教室前边站着一位老师。*A teacher is standing in front of the classroom.* / 桌子上放着书、笔和本子。*Books, pens, and notebooks are placed on the table.* |
| 二57 | Serial Verb Sentence Type 1 | 连动句1：表示前后动作先后发生：他开门出去了。*He opened the door and went out.* / 我们吃完饭去图书馆吧。*Let's go to the library after eating.* |
| 二58 | Comparison Sentence Type 2 | 比较句2：**(1) A 比 B + Adj + Quantity Complement:** 姐姐比我大两岁。*My sister is two years older than me.* / 房间外边比里边凉快一些。*It's a bit cooler outside the room than inside.* **(2) A 比 B + 更/还 + Adj:** 他的手机比我的更贵。*His phone is more expensive than mine.* / 今天比昨天还凉快。*Today is even cooler than yesterday.* **(3) A 不如 B (+ Adj):** 我的中文成绩不如班长。*My Chinese grades aren't as good as the class monitor's.* / 火车不如飞机快。*Trains aren't as fast as planes.* **(4) A 有 B (+ 这么/那么) + Adj:** 你哥哥有你高吗？*Is your brother as tall as you?* / 她家的院子有篮球场那么大。*Her yard is as big as a basketball court.* |

#### A.2.5.3 Complex Sentences (复句)

| # | Pattern | Examples |
|---|---------|----------|
| 二62 | Sequential Complex Sentence | 承接复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 吃了晚饭，我们出去走走。*After dinner, let's go for a walk.* / 他回房间拿了衣服，去教室上课了。*He went to his room to get clothes, then went to class.* **(2) With conjunctions: 先……，再/然后……** 你先去超市买东西，再回家。*First go to the supermarket, then go home.* / 我先去吃午饭，然后回房间休息。*First I'll eat lunch, then rest in my room.* |
| 二63 | Progressive Complex Sentence | 递进复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 那个地方我去过了，去过两次了。*I've been there, twice already.* / 他弟弟会说中文，说得很流利。*His brother can speak Chinese, very fluently.* **(2) With conjunctions: ……，更/还……；不但……，而且……** 昨天很冷，今天更冷了。*It was cold yesterday, even colder today.* / 班长学习很好，还经常帮助同学。*The class monitor studies well and often helps classmates.* / 她不但会说中文，而且说得很好。*Not only can she speak Chinese, but she speaks it well.* |
| 二64 | Alternative Complex Sentence | 选择复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 这次旅行你坐火车，坐飞机？*For this trip, are you taking the train or flying?* / 我们星期六去，星期天去？*Are we going Saturday or Sunday?* **(2) With conjunctions: (是)……，还是……** 你是坐火车来的，还是坐飞机来的？*Did you come by train or plane?* / 周末你们想去打排球，还是想去打篮球？*Do you want to play volleyball or basketball this weekend?* |
| 二65 | Adversative Complex Sentence | 转折复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 这件衣服样子不错，有点儿贵。*This clothes looks nice, but it's a bit expensive.* / 这次去饭店，我们花钱不多，吃得很不错。*At the restaurant, we didn't spend much but ate well.* **(2) With conjunctions: 虽然……，但是/可是……；……，不过……** 那个公园虽然不大，但是非常漂亮。*Although that park is small, it's very beautiful.* / 虽然明天可能下雨，可是我还是想去那儿看看。*Although it might rain tomorrow, I still want to go there.* / 这个房间不太大，不过住着很舒服。*This room isn't big, but it's comfortable to live in.* |
| 二66 | Hypothetical Complex Sentence | 假设复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 明天下雨，我们在家休息。*If it rains tomorrow, we'll rest at home.* / 明天不下雨，我们出去玩儿。*If it doesn't rain tomorrow, we'll go out.* **(2) With conjunctions: 如果……，就……；……的话，就……** 如果你下午有时间，我们就一起去超市吧。*If you have time this afternoon, let's go to the supermarket together.* / 明天天气不好的话，我就不去公园了。*If the weather is bad tomorrow, I won't go to the park.* |
| 二67 | Conditional Complex Sentence (条件复句：只要……，就……) | 只要你认真学习，就一定能取得好成绩。*As long as you study seriously, you'll get good grades.* / 只要你通过这次考试，我就送你一件礼物。*As long as you pass this exam, I'll give you a gift.* |
| 二68 | Causal Complex Sentence | 因果复句：**(1) Without conjunctions:** 我今天太忙了，午饭都没吃。*I was too busy today, I didn't even eat lunch.* / 那个学生病了，没来上课。*That student is sick and didn't come to class.* **(2) With conjunctions: 因为……，所以……** 因为很累，所以我今天不想做饭了。*Because I'm tired, I don't want to cook today.* / 因为明天有考试，所以我想早一点儿睡觉。*Because there's an exam tomorrow, I want to sleep early.* |
| 二69 | Contracted Complex Sentence (紧缩复句：一……就……) | 他一起床就去洗脸。*As soon as he gets up, he washes his face.* / 我一喝酒就脸红。*As soon as I drink, my face turns red.* |

## Proficiency Description

According to the official standard, learners at HSK Level 2 can:

- Understand and use simple Chinese words and sentences to meet basic daily communication needs
- Communicate in simple tasks requiring direct exchange of information on familiar topics
- Describe aspects of their background, immediate environment, and matters in areas of immediate need
