# Grammar Point: 当然 (dāngrán)

| Category | Level | Character | Pinyin | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mood Adverb / Adjective | HSK 3 (三19) | 当然 | dāngrán | of course; certainly; without doubt; natural |

## The Core Concept: "As it Should Be"

**当然** (dāngrán) means "of course."

* **当** (dāng) = match / ought to / should.
* **然** (rán) = thus / like this (suffix forming adverbs/adjectives).

Literally: "It should be like this."

It expresses that something is reasonable, expected, or natural.

## 1. As an Adverb: "Of Course"

Used to strongly affirm a statement or answer a question.

**Structure:** `Subject + 当然 + Verb` OR `当然，Subject + ...`

* **A: 你去参加聚会吗？**
  * *Nǐ qù cānjiā jùhuì ma?*
  * Are you going to the party?
* **B: 当然去！**
  * *Dāngrán qù!*
  * **Of course** I'm going!

* **遇到困难，朋友之间当然应该互相帮助。**
  * *Yùdào kùnnán, péngyou zhījiān dāngrán yīnggāi hùxiāng bāngzhù.*
  * When meeting difficulties, between friends it is **naturally/certainly** right to help each other.

## 2. As an Adjective: "Only Natural"

Used to describe a situation that is expected or logical.

**Structure:** `...是当然的` or `理所当然`

* **取得好成绩，她高兴是当然的。**
  * *Qǔdé hǎo chéngjì, tā gāoxìng shì dāngrán de.*
  * Getting good grades, her being happy is **only natural**.
* **这是理所当然的事。**
  * *Zhè shì lǐsuǒdāngrán de shì.*
  * This is a matter of course (it goes without saying).

## 3. Conjunction Usage: "Of course..., but..."

Used to concede a point before introducing a counter-argument.

**Structure:** `Subject + 当然 + Clause 1, 但是/可是 + Clause 2`

* **学汉语当然很难，但是很有意思。**
  * *Xué Hànyǔ dāngrán hěn nán, dànshì hěn yǒu yìsi.*
  * Learning Chinese is **of course** very hard, **but** it is very interesting.

## Comparison: 当然 (dāngrán) vs. 一定 (yídìng)

| Word | Meaning | Nuance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **一定** | Definitely / Certain | Emphasizes certainty or will/determination. "I *will* do it." |
| **当然** | Of course | Emphasizes logic or expectation. "It *makes sense* to do it." |

* **我一定去。** (I will definitely go.) -> Promise.
* **我当然去。** (Of course I will go.) -> Why wouldn't I?
