# 晶

## Definition

1. **noun** crystal
2. **adjective** bright; brilliant; sparkling

## 1. Core Meaning

(orig.) stars in the sky → brilliant; radiant → clear; transparent → crystal

The character **晶** is composed of three 日 (sun) characters stacked
together, originally representing the twinkling of stars in the night sky.
From this image of multiple points of light came the meaning "brilliant"
or "sparkling," which then extended to describe anything clear and
transparent, eventually settling on "crystal" as its primary meaning.

## 2. Component Analysis

| Component | Pinyin | Meaning | Role                           |
|-----------|--------|---------|--------------------------------|
| 日        | rì     | sun     | Semantic - brightness/light    |
| 日        | rì     | sun     | Semantic - multiplied radiance |
| 日        | rì     | sun     | Semantic - intense brilliance  |

The tripling of 日 creates an ideographic compound (会意字) where the
repetition intensifies the concept of light and brilliance. Three suns
together evoke the image of countless stars twinkling in the night sky.

## 3. Usage Patterns

### A. As a Noun (Crystal)

Refers to crystalline structures or crystal objects.

- 这块==晶==体非常纯净。
  - _Zhè kuài ==jīng==tǐ fēicháng chúnjìng._
  - This ==crystal== is very pure.

- 雪花的结构像美丽的水==晶==。
  - _Xuěhuā de jiégòu xiàng měilì de shuǐ==jīng==._
  - The structure of snowflakes is like beautiful ==crystal==.

### B. As an Adjective (Brilliant/Sparkling)

Describes something bright, clear, or sparkling.

- 她的眼睛==晶==亮==晶==亮的。
  - _Tā de yǎnjīng ==jīng==liàng ==jīng==liàng de._
  - Her eyes are ==sparkling== bright.

- 露珠==晶==莹剔透。
  - _Lùzhū ==jīng==yíng tītòu._
  - The dewdrops are ==crystal== clear.

## 4. Structural Analysis

**晶** functions primarily as a bound morpheme in modern Chinese, rarely
appearing alone. It typically combines with other characters to form:

- Nouns: 水晶 (crystal), 晶体 (crystalline body), 结晶 (crystallization)
- Adjectives: 晶莹 (sparkling), 晶亮 (bright and clear)

The reduplication pattern 晶晶 or AABB pattern 晶晶亮亮 creates emphasis
on the sparkling quality.

## 7. Comparison

### 晶 vs 亮

| Feature  | 晶 (jīng)             | 亮 (liàng)        |
|----------|-----------------------|-------------------|
| Register | Literary/scientific   | Colloquial        |
| Focus    | Sparkling, refractive | General brightness|
| Usage    | Bound morpheme        | Free morpheme     |
| Example  | 晶莹的露珠            | 很亮的灯          |

## 8. Common Errors

### Confusing 晶 with 精

Both are pronounced jīng but have different meanings:

- 晶 (jīng): crystal, sparkling (related to light)
- 精 (jīng): essence, spirit,精 (related to refinement)

❌ 这是一块水精。(wrong character)
✅ 这是一块水晶。(crystal)
