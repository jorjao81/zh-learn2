### A.5.7 Colloquial Patterns (口语格式)

| # | Pattern | Meaning | Examples |
|---|---------|---------|----------|
| 五60 | X也不是，Y也不是 | can't do X, can't do Y (dilemma) | 他这样开玩笑，弄得我哭也不是，笑也不是。*His joking left me not knowing whether to cry or laugh.* / 一看来了这么多人，他紧张得坐也不是，站也不是。*Seeing so many people arrive, he was so nervous he didn't know whether to sit or stand.* / 走也不是，留也不是，真不知道怎么办好。*Can't leave, can't stay—really don't know what to do.* |
| 五61 | X也X不得，Y也Y不得 | can't X, can't Y (impossible situation) | 他腰疼起来的时候站也站不得，坐也坐不得。*When his back hurts, he can neither stand nor sit.* / 孩子大了，骂也骂不得，打也打不得。*The child is grown up; you can't scold them, can't hit them.* / 这件事愁得他吃也吃不得，睡也睡不得。*This matter worried him so much he couldn't eat or sleep.* |
| 五62 | X是它，Y也是它 | Whether X or Y, it's still it (no choice) | 好是它，坏也是它，你没有别的选择。*Good or bad, it's still it—you have no other choice.* / 成功是它，失败也是它，这个选择我绝对不后悔。*Success or failure, it's still it—I absolutely won't regret this choice.* / 等一个小时是它，等两个小时也是它，只能坐这一班车回家了。*Wait one hour or two, it's still it—this is the only bus home.* |

## Proficiency Description

According to the official standard, learners at HSK Level 5 can:

- Use Chinese to discuss relatively complex topics with reasonable fluency
- Read Chinese newspapers and magazines with some difficulty
- Watch Chinese films and TV programs with moderate comprehension
- Give a relatively complete speech in Chinese
